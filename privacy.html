Columbine Golf League
Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 3, 2026

Overview

The Columbine Golf League ("the League") is a recreational golf organization. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information, including phone numbers used for SMS communications related to league activities such as scheduling, payout notifications, and tournament updates.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when members register or participate in the League:

  • Name
  • Phone number (for SMS notifications)
  • Wagering activity within the league's pari-mutuel betting system

How We Use Your Information

Phone numbers are used exclusively for sending SMS messages related to league operations, including:

  • Tournament schedules and updates
  • Bet confirmations and wagering notifications
  • Payout results and winner announcements
  • Important league announcements
We do not use member information for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose unrelated to league activities.

SMS Consent and Opt-In

Members provide their phone number voluntarily when registering for the Columbine Golf League. By providing a phone number at registration, members consent to receive SMS text messages from the League related to the activities described above.

Message frequency will vary based on league activity and tournament schedule.

Opt-Out Instructions

Members may opt out of SMS communications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message from the League
  • Contacting the league administrator directly

After opting out, members will no longer receive SMS messages from the League. To re-enroll, reply START or contact the league administrator.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact the league administrator directly.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share member personal information with third parties, except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • With SMS service providers (e.g., Twilio) solely for the purpose of delivering messages on our behalf

Our SMS provider is bound by their own privacy and data security policies and is not permitted to use member data for any other purpose.

Data Security

Member information is stored securely and access is limited to league administrators. We take reasonable precautions to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. As a recreational non-commercial league, data is not stored in external databases beyond what is necessary to operate the league's scheduling and wagering system.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. The Columbine Golf League is not responsible for any charges applied by your mobile carrier.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Members will be notified of significant changes via SMS or through league communications.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request removal of your information, please contact the Columbine Golf League administrator.

The Columbine Golf League is a private recreational organization and is not affiliated with any commercial entity.
